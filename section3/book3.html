1.zero to one

Zero to One explores how true innovation happens by creating something entirely new rather than simply improving what already exists. Peter Thiel argues that the greatest businesses are built through original ideas that move the world from "zero to one"—creating new products, technologies, or industries instead of competing in crowded markets. Drawing on his experience as the co-founder of PayPal and an early investor in Facebook, Thiel shares valuable insights on startups, competition, leadership, and the future of technology. The book encourages readers to think independently, challenge conventional wisdom, and build businesses that create lasting value.
