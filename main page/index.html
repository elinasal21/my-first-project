why books are so important??

Francis Bacon argues that books and study are valuable because they bring enjoyment, improve conversation, and strengthen judgment. Reading helps people think more clearly, make wiser decisions, and develop knowledge, but it should be balanced with real-life experience. He advises readers not to read simply to argue or accept everything they read, but to carefully consider and evaluate ideas. Bacon also explains that different books serve different purposes—some deserve only a quick look, while others should be read slowly and thoughtfully. His famous conclusion is that reading enriches the mind, discussion sharpens understanding, and writing develops precision, making books essential for intellectual and personal growth.

_Summary of Of Studies by Francis Bacon
